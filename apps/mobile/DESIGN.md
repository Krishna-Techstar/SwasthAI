# SwasthAI Premium Mobile Design System

> ⚠️ **COLOR LAW**: All color decisions are governed by [`COLOR_SYSTEM.md`](./COLOR_SYSTEM.md).
> Do NOT use any color outside that specification. Purple is the ONLY brand identity color.

## Core Aesthetic
- **Style:** iOS inspired premium healthcare application, minimal Apple Health aesthetics.
- **Feel:** Intelligent, calm, futuristic, safe, and medically trustworthy.
- **Visuals:** Soft neumorphism, glassmorphism, purple pastel healthcare gradients, floating cards, clean spacing.

## Typography System
- **Font Family:** Primary: SF Pro Display (or Inter). Fallback: Poppins.
- **Font Weights:** Light: 300, Regular: 400, Medium: 500, SemiBold: 600, Bold: 700.
- **Scale:** Hero Heading: 34px, Screen Title: 28px, Section Title: 20px, Card Heading: 16px, Body Text: 14px, Small Label: 12px, Micro Label: 10px.

## Global Design Rules
### Geometry & Border Radius
- Cards: 24px
- Buttons: 18px
- Inputs: 20px
- Charts: 22px
- Floating Buttons: 999px (Circular)

### Shadows & Depth
- Soft shadow: `0px 10px 30px rgba(139, 92, 246, 0.08)`
- Card shadow: `0px 8px 20px rgba(0,0,0,0.05)`
- Floating action: `0px 15px 40px rgba(139,92,246,0.25)`

### Blur Effects
- Glass blur: `backdrop-filter: blur(18px)`

## Component System
### Buttons
- **Primary Button:** Purple gradient background, rounded full (18px), white text, elevated shadow.
- **Secondary Button:** White background, light border (`#E5E7EB`), dark text.

### Card Design
- **Structure:** Rounded 24px, White background (`#FFFFFF`), Soft shadow, 20px padding.
- **Content Flow:** Small icon, large metric, trend indicator, status label.

## Layout & Spacing
- **Grid:** 8pt spacing system.
- **Padding:** Horizontal 20px, Vertical 16px.
- **Card Gaps:** 16px.

## UI Specifics
- Use soft waveform animations and floating layered surfaces.
- Ambient background gradients (using Soft Lavender to Extra Light Lavender).
- Ensure high clinical readability without being cluttered. No generic EMR aesthetics.
